====================================
The ``5-text_indentation`` module
====================================

Importing the function:
    >>> text_indentation = __import__('5-text_indentation').text_indentation

Normal usage:
    >>> text_indentation("Hello. How are you? I am fine: thanks.")
    Hello.
    <BLANKLINE>
    How are you?
    <BLANKLINE>
    I am fine:
    <BLANKLINE>
    thanks.

Handling multiple spaces after delimiters:
    >>> text_indentation("Sentence one.   Sentence two?    Final.")
    Sentence one.
    <BLANKLINE>
    Sentence two?
    <BLANKLINE>
    Final.

Non-string input:
    >>> text_indentation(12345)
